RetroFix is an automation platform designed to make it easy and intuitive for anyone to automate the boring, manual work they hate doing.

## Powered by Reggie

Everyone using RetroFix has access to **Reggie**, our AI workflow agent. Reggie allows you to interact with RetroFix using just plain English. He is able to create, test, modify, and fix all of your workflows for you. As he is AI, he is available **24/7** to make it as easy as possible for you to automate anything you would like.

<Note>
  Reggie learns from your feedback and continuously improves at understanding your automation needs.
</Note>
